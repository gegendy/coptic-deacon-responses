# Vespers and Matins - Contents (Coptic Reader Edition)

## Vespers

1. Stand up for prayer
2. Let us pray
3. Tovhina
4. Litanies (departed, sick, travelers)
5. Gospel call and stand in fear
6. Major evening litanies

## Matins

1. Stand up for prayer
2. Let us pray
3. Bow your heads to the Lord
4. Let us attend in the fear of God
5. Saved. Amen.
